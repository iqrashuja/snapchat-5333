In the vast landscape of social media platforms, Snapchat remains a dominant player with its unique features that cater to the younger generation. The app's core functionality revolves around the concept of "snaps," which are short-lived photos or videos that users can share with their friends or post to their "stories." With the ability to add filters, stickers, and captions to their snaps, users can personalize their content and showcase their creativity. One of Snapchat's standout features is the ephemeral nature of these snaps, which disappear after being viewed, adding an element of spontaneity and excitement to the platform.

Snapchat's "story" feature has revolutionized how users share moments from their day in a more cohesive and interactive manner. By compiling snaps into a chronological narrative that disappears after 24 hours, users can create a visual diary of their experiences that provides a snapshot of their daily lives. The story feature encourages users to share multiple snaps throughout their day, giving their friends and followers a real-time glimpse into what they are up to. With the ability to add text, drawings, and filters to their story snaps, users can craft engaging and dynamic visual narratives that capture the essence of each moment.
