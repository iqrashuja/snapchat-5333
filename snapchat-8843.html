Basic Instructions for Using Snapchat Profile

To start with Snapchat, install the app from your device's app store. Launch the app after it finishes installing. Create a new account by entering your email address, creating a password, and filling in your birthdate. Next, pick a username that you like and that is not already taken. Include a profile picture and fill out your profile information.

After you have set up your account, take some time to explore the different features of Snapchat. Experiment with sending a snap to a friend by taking a photo or video, adding a caption or filter, and then selecting the friend you want to send it to. Discover the different filters, lenses, and stickers available to add some fun to your snaps. Keep in mind to adjust your privacy settings to control who can send you snaps and view your story.
