Beginner's Instructions for Setting Up Snapchat Account

Get started with Snapchat, get the app from your device's app store. Open the app after it finishes installing. Make a new account by entering your email address, creating a password, and filling in your birthdate. Then, select a username that you like and that is not already taken. Include a profile picture and fill out your profile information.

Once you've set up your account, take some time to explore the different features of Snapchat. Try sending a snap to a friend by taking a photo or video, adding a caption or filter, and then selecting the friend you want to send it to. Explore the different filters, lenses, and stickers available to add some fun to your snaps. Don't forget to adjust your privacy settings to control who can send you snaps and view your story.
