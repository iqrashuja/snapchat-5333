Snapchat for Beginners

Basic Instructions for Setting Up Snapchat Account

To start with Snapchat, get the app from your device's app store. Start the app after it finishes installing. Set up a new account by entering your email address, creating a password, and filling in your birthdate. After that, select a username that you like and that is not already taken. Include a profile picture and fill out your profile information.

Once you have set up your account, take some time to explore the different features of Snapchat. Experiment with sending a snap to a friend by taking a photo or video, adding a caption or filter, and then selecting the friend you want to send it to. Check out the different filters, lenses, and stickers available to add some fun to your snaps. Keep in mind to adjust your privacy settings to control who can send you snaps and view your story.
