Basic Instructions for Starting Snapchat Account

Begin with Snapchat, download the app from your device's app store. Open the app after it finishes installing. Create a new account by entering your email address, creating a password, and filling in your birthdate. Next, pick a username that you like and that is not already taken. Once you have set up your account, take some time to explore the different features of Snapchat.
