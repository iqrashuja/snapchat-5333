Basic Instructions for Using Snapchat Account

Begin with Snapchat, download the app from your device's app store. Launch the app after it finishes installing. Set up a new account by entering your email address, creating a password, and filling in your birthdate. After that, pick a username that you like and that is not already taken. Put in a profile picture and fill out your profile information.

After you have set up your account, take some time to explore the different features of Snapchat. Explore the different filters, lenses, and stickers available to add some fun to your snaps. Remember to adjust your privacy settings to control who can send you snaps and view your story.
